# SendGrid (Twilio)

SendGrid is our transactional email provider, handling all system emails, notifications, and customer communications.

## Why SendGrid

- **Deliverability**: Industry-leading inbox placement rates
- **Scale**: Handles billions of emails; no capacity concerns
- **Twilio ecosystem**: Integrates with our existing Twilio relationship
- **Pricing**: Competitive per-email pricing with generous free tier

## How We Use It

| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| All products | Account verification, password reset |
| Murphy | Delivery alerts, weekly reports |
| P4gent | Supplier communication drafts, renewal reminders |
| Nomos Cloud | Usage alerts, compliance notifications |
| SmartBoxes | Deployment notifications, capability updates |

## Risk Considerations

- **Dependency**: Non-critical (degraded experience, not outage)
- **Mitigation**: Can fall back to direct SMTP or alternative providers
- **Cost**: ~$0.001 per email at scale
