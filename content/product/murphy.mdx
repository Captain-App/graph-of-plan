# Murphy

Murphy is an AI-powered operations assistant for infrastructure and DevOps.

## What it is

Murphy is an autonomous agent that:

- Monitors infrastructure and responds to incidents
- Executes remediation within SmartBox boundaries
- Interacts with systems through Nomos-generated interfaces
- Learns from operational patterns to prevent future issues

## Target users

- DevOps and SRE teams
- Platform engineering organisations
- Companies with complex infrastructure requiring 24/7 monitoring
