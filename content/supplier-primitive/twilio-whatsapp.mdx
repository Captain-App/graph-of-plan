# Twilio WhatsApp Business API

Official WhatsApp integration for business messaging.

## What It Is

The Twilio WhatsApp Business API provides programmatic access to WhatsApp's messaging platform. It supports text messages, media, interactive buttons, and template messages for notifications.

## How We Use It

WhatsApp becomes an interface for SmartBox interaction:

- **Conversational control**: Send commands to your SmartBox via WhatsApp
- **Status updates**: Receive deployment notifications and alerts
- **Approval workflows**: Approve or reject actions with quick reply buttons
- **File sharing**: Send and receive files, screenshots, and logs
