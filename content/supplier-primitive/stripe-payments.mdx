# Stripe Payments

Stripe Payments is the core payment processing API for accepting cards, bank transfers, and local payment methods.

## Capabilities

- Card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank debits (ACH, SEPA, BACS)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Local payment methods by region

## How We Use It

Primary payment acceptance across all products. Customers pay via Stripe Checkout or embedded payment forms.
