# Stripe Connect

Stripe Connect enables marketplace payments, allowing us to facilitate payments between customers and third-party creators.

## Capabilities

- Multi-party payments
- Automated payouts to connected accounts
- Platform fee collection
- 1099 tax reporting for US creators
