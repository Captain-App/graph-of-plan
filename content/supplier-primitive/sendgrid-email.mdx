# SendGrid Email API

SendGrid Email API is a transactional email service for sending system-generated emails at scale.

## Capabilities

- Transactional email delivery
- Email templates and personalization
- Delivery tracking and analytics
- Suppression list management
- Dedicated IP options for enterprise

## How We Use It

All system emails across products: account verification, password resets, alerts, reports, and notifications. P4gent also uses it for sending supplier communication drafts on behalf of users.
