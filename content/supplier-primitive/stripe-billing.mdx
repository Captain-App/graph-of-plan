# Stripe Billing

Stripe Billing handles subscription management, usage-based pricing, and invoicing.

## Capabilities

- Subscription lifecycle management
- Usage-based metering and billing
- Proration and plan changes
- Invoice generation and tax calculation
- Customer portal for self-service

## How We Use It

| Product | Billing Model |
|---------|---------------|
| Murphy | Per-team subscription tiers |
| SmartBoxes | Credits-based PAYG + capability subscriptions |
| Nomos Cloud | Usage-based (entries stored, queries executed) |
| P4gent | Per-user subscription tiers |
