# Murphy Design Partners

Working with select agency partners to validate the delivery prediction model.

## Scope

- Integration with Jira/Linear/GitHub
- Basic delivery prediction algorithm
- Weekly report generation
- Slack notifications for risk alerts

## Success Criteria

- 3-5 design partner agencies
- Predictions accurate within 1 week 70% of time
- Partners using reports in client meetings
- Clear feedback loop for iteration

## Design Partner Selection

Looking for:
- Agencies with 5-20 person teams
- Running complex projects (not simple maintenance)
- Willing to share anonymized data
- Can commit to weekly feedback sessions
