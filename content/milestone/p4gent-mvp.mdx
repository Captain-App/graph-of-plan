# P4gent MVP

A working purchasing assistant that tracks suppliers and helps with procurement.

## Scope

- Supplier database management
- Spend tracking and categorization
- Basic email drafting with context
- Invoice parsing and matching

## Success Criteria

- Can track 50+ suppliers per user
- Correctly categorizes 80%+ of transactions
- Email drafts save 5+ minutes per email
- Invoice matching accuracy > 90%

## Core Features

1. **Supplier CRM**: Contact details, history, notes
2. **Spend Dashboard**: By category, supplier, time
3. **Smart Compose**: Context-aware email drafting
4. **Invoice OCR**: Extract and match to POs
