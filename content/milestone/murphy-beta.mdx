# Murphy Public Beta

Expanding beyond design partners to self-serve customers.

## Scope

- Self-service onboarding
- Improved prediction accuracy from partner feedback
- Additional integrations (Asana, Monday, ClickUp)
- Team dashboards and historical analysis

## Success Criteria

- 25+ active teams
- Prediction accuracy > 75%
- Self-serve conversion rate > 10%
- NPS > 40

## Key Learning Goals

- Which project types benefit most?
- What prediction horizon is most useful?
- How do teams actually use the predictions?
