# Brex

Brex is a corporate financial services platform providing credit cards, expense management, banking, and spend controls for startups and enterprises.

## Overview

**Company**: Brex (San Francisco)
**Founded**: 2017
**Funding**: $1.2B+ (Series D, Tiger Global, Y Combinator)
**Pricing**: Free tier (Essentials), Premium at $12/user/mo

## Why They're Not a Competitor

**Brex is corporate banking for VC-backed startups. P4gent is an AI assistant for freelancers.**

| | Brex | P4gent |
|---|------|--------|
| **Customer** | Series A+ startups | Freelancers and solopreneurs |
| **Core product** | Banking + corporate cards | AI spending assistant |
| **Value prop** | High-limit cards, no personal guarantee | "Catch forgotten subscriptions" |
| **Minimum bar** | Funded company | Anyone with suppliers |

### The Core Difference

Brex sells status and infrastructure to funded startups. P4gent sells time savings to time-poor individuals.

A Brex customer:
- Has raised venture capital
- Needs corporate banking
- Wants high credit limits without personal guarantees
- Has finance operations to manage

A P4gent customer:
- Freelancer or solo founder
- Uses personal bank accounts
- Wants help tracking subscriptions and suppliers
- Has no finance team (they are the finance team)
